Cookies

A cookie is a piece of data stored on the user’s computer tied to information about the user. Usage of a cookie is in no way linked to any personally identifiable information while on our site. We use persistent cookies. A persistent cookie is a small text file stored on the user’s hard drive for an extended period of time. The persistent cookies that are being set on our site may look as though they are cookies being set by https://sellbroke.com however these cookies may actually be Google Adwords and/or Google Analytics cookies. We use Google Adwords and Google Analytics to monitor traffic on our site, Google Adwords and Google Analytics cookies are not linked to your personal information in any way. Cookies are a perfectly normal part of Internet operations. With most Internet Browsers, you can erase cookies from your computer hard drive, block all cookies, or receive a warning before a cookie is stored on your computer. Persistent cookies can be removed by following your Internet browser’s help file directions. We use session ID cookies to make it easier for you to navigate our site. A session ID cookie expires when you close your browser. If you reject cookies, you may still use our site, but your ability to use some areas of our site may be limited. Third Party Cookies The use of cookies by our service providers is not covered by our privacy statement. We do not have access or control over these cookies. Our tracking utility company use session ID cookies to improve our advertising. This privacy statement covers the use of cookies by sellbroke.com only and does not cover the use of cookies by any other third party.
